Is Veda Trader free?

Access is free once you complete a verified $10 deposit at our partner broker. The deposit stays in your wallet — we don't charge a subscription.

How many signals will I get per session?

Typically 5 to 7+ high-quality signals per session, depending on market conditions. Quality always beats quantity — we'd rather skip a session than send you noise.

What is a "Gale" time?

Gale is a recovery sequence used in binary-options scalping. Our 2-minute primary trade has two backup windows (1st Gale at 4 min, 2nd Gale at 6 min) so you can manage entries that need a moment to confirm.

Where do the signals come from?

A purpose-built Python scanner runs every 5 minutes against live forex data, applying EMA / MACD / RSI confirmation and ATR-ranked volatility filters. Signals are dispatched to Telegram only when all gates align.

What happens after the trade closes?

Two minutes after entry, the bot re-checks price and stamps each signal as a real WIN or LOSS — broadcast to the channel as a green or red bar so you always know where the strategy stands.
